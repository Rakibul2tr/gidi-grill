Mission

To Humbly Serve
Give back

Our mission is to open hearts and minds to our unique flavours and share them between the cultures of West Africa, The Caribbean and Scotland to unite people with delicious, homely food. We want to provide an unforgettable experience that is open to everyone regardless of gender identity, origin, age and social position.

Food as it should be and how it should taste. It should feel good. It should do good things for you and the world around you.Do right and far exceed expectations daily. We do the right thing and lead by putting people first.

Everything we do is about you. From chefs who create exciting new flavours, to crew members who know exactly how you want your drink – we prioritise what you need to get you on your way. We strive to keep you at your best, and we remain loyal to you, your tastes and your time.

Our opportunity to create an experience that FEEDs – Far Exceeds Expectations Daily – and show how much we care.
